# Tableau Projects

This folder contains Tableau projects designed to demonstrate program evaluation, reporting, and data visualization skills in public-sector and government-adjacent contexts.

## Tableau Public Profile
https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/lynn.clemons/vizzes

## Projects

### 1) World Happiness Demo
- **Viz Link:** https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/lynn.clemons/viz/WorldHappinessDemo_17683320243340/Sheet2
- **Tools:** Tableau, Excel
- **Summary:** This dashboard explores global happiness indicators to examine how economic, social, and governance-related factors correlate with reported happiness scores across countries and regions.
- **Key Focus Areas:**
  - Comparative analysis of happiness scores across countries and regions.
  - Exploration of contributing factors such as GDP per capita, social support, and perceptions of government effectiveness.
  - Identification of broad patterns and disparities relevant to policy, quality-of-life, and social outcome discussions.

> Note: This visualization is exploratory in nature and is intended to demonstrate data visualization, comparative analysis, and interpretability skills using publicly available data.

### 2) CO₂ Emissions per Capita — Regional Trend Analysis
- **Viz Link:** https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/lynn.clemons/viz/CO2Demo_17683341367010/CO2PerCapitabyRegion
- **Tools:** Tableau
- **Summary:** This dashboard analyzes trends in CO₂ emissions per capita across global regions to support a comparative assessment of environmental impact, regional disparities, and long-term sustainability considerations.
- **Key Focus Areas:**
  - Regional comparison of per-capita CO₂ emissions over time
  - Identification of long-term trends and divergence across regions
  - Contextual insights relevant to environmental policy, accountability, and sustainability reporting


  > Note: This visualization uses publicly available environmental data and is intended to demonstrate trend analysis and comparative reporting techniques commonly applied in public-sector and policy-support contexts.
